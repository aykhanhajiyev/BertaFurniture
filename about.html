Who We Are

It is almost a decade that Berta Furniture & Accessories LLC is building its reputation as a reliable supplier and importer in USA. From the beginning of 2010 company incorporated and started it is operations in Brooklyn, New York. Company with its newly established warehouse facilities in New Jersey serves New York area directly to projects and to hardware outlet retailers.

Nowadays, company became a reliable supplier of many cabinet and kitchen makers in New York area. In line with direct sales as an importer and wholesaler company work with main retailers and serves to individuals via their network.

All products that company is providing to its customers are produced for Berta exclusively at approved manufacturing locations all over the world.

Company plans to establish a reliable network and serve to customers in all over the US via its own network of potential partners. In line with the product range that company currently offering it offers different type of services to enhance and deliver true value to its customers which company views as a reliable future partners.
